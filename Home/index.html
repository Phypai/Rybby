About Us

We breed authentic pure bred Cane Corso's. Our goal is to maintain the purity of this breed, as well as other breeds. We breed for structure and amazing personalities. The breed was brought to my attention when I was living alone and wanted protection. This breed is fierce and loyal. Not for the faint of heart. Even though they are fierce, they are huge babies and just want cuddles from their owners. Our family currently consists of Baeu, Xena, Annaliese, and Henry. Henry is a kitten and Annaliese is a dachshund. Xena and Baeu are both Cane Corso's.
